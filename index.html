Intel

Intel is a technology company founded by Gordon Moore and Robert Royce on July 18, 1968. It is the world's second largest and second highest valued semiconductor chip makers. Its headquarters are in Santa Clara, Californisa. Intel makes and supplies procesors to computer manufacturers. Intel is renowned for the processor chips that they create. These processors are utilised in many modern computers such as those made by Dell and HP

Infotainement system

Intel will be manufacturing the infortainment ststem for the new Tesla model 3. This screen in the centre of the dashboard is responsible for displaying navigation directions, speed and auto pilot. The infotainment system in the model 3, as previously stated, will be made by intel rather than the company Tesla previously used to produce the system. The infotainment system has many features such as radio, internet, onboard maps and navigation, voice activated control buttons, bluetooth, a 15 inch touch screen, dual zone climate control system, 2 usb ports, keyless entry and remote climate control using tesla app, voice activated control.
